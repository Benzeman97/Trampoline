Make your life simpler.

Are you developing an application based on microservices philosofy using spring boot? Are you tired of that set of scripts to make it be up&running? Quiet, Trampoline has come into your life.

Multiplatform

Fully tested on Mac OS and Windows, it will allow you to keep working on your favorite Operating System.

Persistent

No need to reintroduce your data. Once information has been registered, it will be available all the time.

Development

It will speed up your development since you will not have to open a terminal or create scripts to start your microservices.
